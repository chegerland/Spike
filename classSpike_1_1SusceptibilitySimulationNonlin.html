void Spike::SusceptibilitySimulationNonlin::calculate ( size_t  trials)

Calculates the linear and nonlinear susceptibilities for a given number of trials. Each time the white noise signal gets generated again, the neuron produces a new spike train and then the susceptibilities are calculated. The result is then added (!) to the susceptibility vectors.
